Boston Police Incident Data Story

This project explores the most common incident types in the Boston Police dataset and shows how their frequency changes throughout the day. Click any bar in the first chart to update the second chart and view the hourly pattern for that incident type.

Storyline: Common police incidents include both crime-related and service-related events, and these incidents occur at different times of the day depending on their type.
